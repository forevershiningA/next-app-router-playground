## Technical Implementation

### Detection Priority

1. **Long verse phrases first** (most specific)
   - "for god so loved the world that he gave his only begotten son"
   
2. **Medium phrases** (if long not found)
   - "for god so loved the world"
   
3. **Short distinctive phrases** (fallback)
   - "the son sets free"

### Smart Matching

- Case-insensitive
- Handles variations (thee/you, etc.)
- Removes punctuation
- Matches partial phrases

### Length Management

- Phrases >50 chars become sole slug (no motifs added)
- Phrases 30-50 chars may add one motif
- Phrases <30 chars add motifs as needed
