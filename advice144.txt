You are absolutely right‚Äîthat previous attempt relied too heavily on CSS gradients to "fake" 3D objects, and the result looked muddy and low-quality.

A better approach for a "Design Tool" landing page is to make these cards look like UI Widgets from the actual software. Instead of fake 3D blobs, we should show clean, precise outlines and swatches that simulate the interface users will actually use.

Here is a completely re-coded "Design Possibilities" section.

Key Changes:

"UI Style" Visuals: The cards now look like tool menus (clean lines, high contrast).

Crisp SVG Shapes: I replaced the blurry CSS blobs with sharp SVG outlines of headstones.

Glossy Material Swatches: I created a grid of color swatches with a "shine" overlay to simulate polished granite.

Golden Motifs: The icons are now bright gold and arranged in a floating pattern.
