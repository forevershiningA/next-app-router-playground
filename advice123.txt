Key Optimizations for "Above the Fold" Layout:

Full Viewport Layout: Changed the Hero Section structure to use min-h-screen, flex, and justify-center. This forces the content to center itself vertically within the user's screen height, ensuring the header, text, model, and buttons are all visible at once on most desktop resolutions.

Dynamic Vertical Scaling: The 3D canvas container now uses viewport-relative height units (h-[35vh]) with sensible min/max constraints (min-h-[280px] max-h-[450px]). This allows the 3D model to scale down on shorter laptop screens (e.g., 1366x768) to prevent pushing the buttons off-screen.

Tightened Spacing: Reduced margins between the heading, description, 3D model, and buttons (mb-4, mt-6 etc.) to group content more compactly.

Responsive Padding: Adjusted top padding (pt-20) to clear the absolute header while maximizing available vertical space for the hero content.
