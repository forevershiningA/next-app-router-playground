### 3) Fix the “vertical band” glare on the face

Your current light stack (ambient 0.3, key spot 3.0, rim 2.0, env “city” 0.8) is hot; the combo creates those bands. So: lower env, lower key, keep a gentle cool rim. 

```diff
- <ambientLight intensity={0.3} color="#cce0ff" />
+ <ambientLight intensity={0.25} color="#cfe0ff" />

- <spotLight color="#fff5e6" intensity={3.0} angle={0.5} penumbra={1} position={[-2,5,6]} ... />
+ <spotLight color="#ffd8a4" intensity={1.7} angle={0.55} penumbra={0.9} position={[-2.2,4.8,5.5]} castShadow shadow-bias={-0.0001} />

- <spotLight color="#bfdbfe" intensity={2.0} position={[-5,4,-5]} />
+ <spotLight color="#bcd5ff" intensity={0.55} position={[3,2.5,-3]} />

- <Environment preset="city" environmentIntensity={0.8} background={false} environmentRotation={[0, Math.PI/2, 0]} />
+ <Environment preset="studio" environmentIntensity={0.35} background={false} />
```

Tip: If the base still shows a bright “flash,” raise its material roughness slightly in your headstone materials.
