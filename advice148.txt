This is a brilliant creative direction. Instead of a generic grid, we will transform the "Design Possibilities" section into a giant, interactive "Anatomy of a Headstone".

We will build a large, grey granite monument in the center of the screen. The text and images on the monument will actually be the feature descriptions.

The Family Name becomes the Typography feature.

The Motifs become the Art Library feature.

The Stone Shape represents the Shapes library.

The Base represents the Materials selection.
