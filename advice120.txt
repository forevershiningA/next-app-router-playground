The "div extending" issue is caused by a Layout Shift (CLS) mismatch between your loading state and the final loaded 3D canvas.

The Mismatch: Your loader is 480px height (fixed), but your canvas container is min-h-[400px] (fluid). When the 3D component loads, the container snaps from 480px down to 400px (or vice versa), causing the page content below to jump up or down.

The Fix: We need to enforce a strict, consistent height for both the loading state and the final canvas state. I have standardized it to 500px to give the headstone breathing room.

Here is the corrected HomeSplash.tsx.
